UIU Robotics Club

Our mission is to build a progressive platform for Robotics enthusiasts by active
participation in research, training and contest. We do it by organizing various programs,
workshops and competitions that help us create connections among students,
teachers and employers.
The vision of this team is to expand the space robotics research in Bangladesh and demonstrate high-quality
robotic systems in reputed international competitions during the growth.
